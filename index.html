Frequently Asked Questions

How do the online classes work?

We use Zoom with interactive whiteboards and shared digital materials. Classes are recorded so you can review them later, and you'll have 24/7 access to our learning portal.

What does the free assessment include?

The 20-minute session includes a speaking evaluation, a quick grammar check, and a personalized roadmap suggesting which course and timeline fits your goals.

Are materials included in the price?

Yes, all digital textbooks, worksheets, and practice exams are included. You don't need to purchase anything else.
